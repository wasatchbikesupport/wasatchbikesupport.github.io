About

We created Wasatch Bike Support to solve a common problem we saw in most bike shops: when you're in the market for a new bike, you're a VIP. But when it comes time to service or repair your bike, you can sometimes feel like a second class citizen. At WBS, you can skip the hard sell on a new bike - in fact, we don't sell new bikes at all. Instead, we employ only professional mechanics with years of industry experience whose sole mission is to keep your bike in prime condition.

WBS mechanics boast resumes that include team mechanic for the DNA Cycling women's professional team, traveling around the world to provide on the spot support for stage races including trans-rockies, and other major gigs. Our customers frequently provide positive feedback about the professionalism of our service on Google and Yelp.
